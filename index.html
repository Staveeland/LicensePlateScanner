About the App

Bilskilt Scanner is an advanced license plate recognition app that allows users to scan vehicle license plates in real time. The app automatically analyzes passing vehicles and provides information on their status, including whether they have a valid EU inspection.

Simply place your phone on the dashboard or windshield, and the app will scan traffic as it passes by. If a car without a valid EU inspection is detected, you will receive an alert.
